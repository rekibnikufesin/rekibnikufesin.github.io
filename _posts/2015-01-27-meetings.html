It could be just that I’m getting old, but I’m finding myself less tolerant of people wasting my time.

</a>

I use a number of strategies to get out of meetings, depending on the circumstances. Sometimes, it’s as simple as a “Fuck off”. No further explanation needed (see follow-up notes below). Other times, I think I can actually explain away my distaste for meetings logically. Feel free to adapt the following for your own use, and claim back part of your life.

</a>

Consider this: at most tech startups, you can very</b> conservatively estimate each employee is worth $100/revenue. In an eight person meeting, lasting 1 hour- you just blew $800 of your startup’s cash. Are you really in a position to do that?
Maybe so.
Would you spend $800 of your startups cash with getting a quote and turning in a receipt? I doubt it. So it’s not really out of line for me to ask for a meeting agenda and follow up notes, is it? Aren’t these the equivalent of a quote and receipt? If you can’t produce those documents at the appropriate time, it’s unlikely you’ll see me at your meeting.

That strategy actually works quite well for formal meetings, but not so well for the guy or girl who just “likes to grab a quick minute of your time” but does so all too frequently. 


</a>
I like to explain it like this: I get 24 hours each day. No more, no less. They are mine to do with as I choose, but I only get 24 of them. Now here you are, wanting to take one of them from me. I can make more coffee, I can make more money, I can make more witty jokes, but I cannot make more time so whatever you have to say to me should be so important that it is worth that same amount of time in my life that I will never get back.
Taylor Swift should write a song about this.
It may be selfish. Ok, it really is selfish, but it’s true. My 24 hours on this earth each day is the only commodity I can’t negotiate, so no- you are not entitled to any of it.</p>


</a>There will still be persistent people in your life that can’t quite wrap their heads around this. Future, more elaborate explanations won’t help. For these people, unless the individual in question has the authority to fire you on the spot, fall back to the standby #1: Fuck off.
It’s your life, you’re entitled to it.</p>
